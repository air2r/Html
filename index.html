Dew Points

Customer spends specific local currency amount to collect ‘Dew Points”. These are Crypto coins, which can be redeemed by any business anywhere in the world making them truly Global loyalty points.

Dew Rewards

Encourages your customers to be loyal by offering incentives to do repeat business with you. For example, Buy five burger & sixth on house, FREE!!!

Redeem & Enjoy

Redeem your Dew Crypto points for a wide selection of products or purchase in any participating business store across the globe.

Loyalty for all customers

Sign up customers to loyalty program directly using their phone number or email address. Dew Points / Rewards are automatically awarded via SMS/text or email. No need to issue plastic card or Dewber app download. Alternatively, ask customers to check-in at store using the free Dewber App.

Global Loyalty Points and business specific rewards

Dew loyalty points are truly Global loyalty points and can be awarded by any business, anywhere in the world. They can also be redeemed by any business as per business promotion offer. Ex. Business awards - “Spend $1 and get 1 Dew point” OR Redemption as “Get a coffee for 100 Dew Points”

Branding - Business own branded digital loyalty wallet

No need to issues paper cards, Provision to add Business logo and colour scheme provides business branded loyalty wallet in customers’ pockets.

Optimize your expense on ineffective discounts

Don’t just give instant discounts and rebates. Make customers return in future by using bonus loyalty points and rewards.

Multilocation - Supports multiple locations

Business having chain of stores , can run Dewber loyalty program across all locations and customers can receive dew points/ awards OR redeem them at any of business location.

Data - Collect & Track Customer Data

Capture customer phone number / emails, and collect real-time customer data like visit history, points, awards, age and gender. Measure business loyalty program's success within your own dashboard on web, iOS or Android apps.
