How It Works

The platform, Protocol & Token

Global Digital loyalty platform integrating API and blockchain to rewards loyalty token called “Dew Coins”.

Loyalty Ecosystem

Enable SME to run own global rewards & points based loyalty program. Crypto Dew points distribution by business and adoption by consumers will develop loyalty cryptocurrency (Dew coin) ecosystem.

Earn on every Purchases

Business create & launch loyalty in just 5 minutes. Customer registration via mobile phone and when spends specific local currency amount get awarded "Dew crypto coins” & / or“Dew rewards” against each purchase.

Redeem & Enjoy

Redeem your Dew Crypto points for a wide selection of products or purchase in any participating business store across the globe.
